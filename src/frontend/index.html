GreekTax

Unofficial tax estimation toolkit for Greece

Overview

Follow these quick steps to receive a bilingual summary of your Greek income taxes.

  1. Choose the tax year and confirm the language above.
  2. Enable the income sections that match your situation.
  3. Enter the amounts requested for each active section.
  4. Press Calculate to view your results and download a copy.

  • Guided calculator inputs

    Toggle income types as you need them, with inline hints and validation.

  • Bilingual header controls

    Use the top controls to switch between English and Greek without losing your data.

  • Visual income breakdown

    Follow taxes, contributions, and take-home pay through a colour-coded Sankey diagram.

Disclaimer: This tool is unofficial and provided as-is. Inputs are stored locally on your device for up to two hours and are never sent to a server. Please consult a professional accountant for formal filings.

Advanced options

Add EFKA amounts you pay directly outside payroll (for example, voluntary top-ups). Most employees will leave this at 0 because payroll already covers EFKA.

Uncheck to see tax-only net income. EFKA amounts (employee and employer) will be treated as zero in the results.

Provide the annual gross salary if you are paid once per year; otherwise switch to monthly/per payment mode above.

Enter the gross amount per payslip (monthly or bonus). The calculator multiplies it by the payments per year you selected.

Enter PAYE income tax already withheld on payslips to offset the balance due.

Net-to-gross salary conversion isn't supported. Please enter gross amounts only.

Freelance income
Freelance help

Report gross turnover before VAT and declare expenses only when you have invoices (rent, utilities, equipment, etc.).

Selecting an EFKA class preloads mandatory social security payments; you can override the totals with actual receipts if they differ.

Include total invoices issued before VAT or withholding taxes.

List documented operating costs (rent, utilities, equipment, professional fees) that meet AADE deductibility rules.

Social security contributions

Select an EFKA class to preload monthly payments or override them with manual totals.

Mandatory EFKA and auxiliary payments reduce taxable profit euro-for-euro when supported by receipts.

The statutory trade fee is currently abolished for this year.

Agricultural income

Check if you are registered as a professional farmer for the tax year to apply any available reliefs.
